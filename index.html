Still have questions? Talk to us →
Will the AI sound robotic to my clients?

Not at all. Your AI is trained on your brand voice, services, and communication style. It uses natural, warm, conversational language. Clients describe it as friendly and professional — and consistently can't tell it's automated.

Can it actually book appointments?

Yes. The AI offers real time slots inside the conversation, collects client details, and places a reservation hold. Your team receives an immediate notification and confirms — the entire booking happens within the DM thread.

Does it work with Instagram DMs?

Instagram DMs are the primary channel. The AI replies to every DM automatically within seconds, 24/7. It handles pricing, treatment questions, booking requests, and follow-ups — all within the Instagram message thread.

Does it work with WhatsApp?

Absolutely. We integrate with the WhatsApp Business API so your AI responds to WhatsApp inquiries with the same speed and intelligence as Instagram DMs.

Can it answer specific service questions?

Yes — this is one of the most powerful features. During onboarding we train the AI on your full service menu: Botox, dermal fillers, laser hair removal, skin treatments, chemical peels, body contouring, and more — including pricing, duration, downtime, and pre/post care for each.

How long does setup take?

After your onboarding call, your custom AI assistant is typically live within 48 hours. We handle all technical setup — channel integrations, training, testing. You fill out a detailed onboarding form; we do the rest.
